get_max_depolarization_per_ms
=============================


.. py:function:: data_base.db_initializers.prepare_ANN_batches.get_max_depolarization_per_ms(vt_dask)

   Subsample a voltage trace dask dataframe to 1ms bins based on the maximum depolarization.

   :Parameters: **vt\_dask** (*dask.dataframe*) -- Voltage traces dask dataframe

   :returns: A dask dataframe of the maximum depolarization per ms.
   :rtype: dask.dataframe

   .. attention:: This method assumes a voltage trace time interval of 0.025 ms.
