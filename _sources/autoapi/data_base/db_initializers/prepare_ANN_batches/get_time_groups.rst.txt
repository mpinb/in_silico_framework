get_time_groups
===============


.. py:function:: data_base.db_initializers.prepare_ANN_batches.get_time_groups(vt_pandas)

   Get the time groups for binning voltage traces.

   Group the voltage trace in 1 ms bins, assuming a time interval of 0.025 ms.

   :Parameters: **vt\_pandas** (*pd.DataFrame*) -- Voltage traces dataframe.

   :returns: A list of time groups.
   :rtype: list

   .. attention:: This method assumes a time interval of 0.025 ms.
