get_st_from_spike_times_and_stim_times
======================================


.. py:function:: spike_analysis.core.get_st_from_spike_times_and_stim_times(spike_times, stim_times, offset=0, mode=&#39;spike_times&#39;)

   Computes spike times dataframe based on list of spike times and stimulus times.

   :Parameters: * **spike\_times** (*list*) -- List of spike times.
                * **stim\_times** (*list*) -- List of stimulus times.
                * **offset** (*float, optional*) -- Offset value. Defaults to 0.
                * **mode** (*str, optional*) -- Mode of computation. Can be 'spike\_times' or 'ISIs'. Defaults to 'spike\_times'.

   :returns: Pandas dataframe. Its format is as follows: One row is one trial. The columns contain the spike times
             and are named in ascending order (0,1,2, ...).
   :rtype: pd.DataFrame

   :raises ValueError: If mode is not 'spike\_times' or 'ISIs'.
   :raises ValueError: If offset is not negative.
