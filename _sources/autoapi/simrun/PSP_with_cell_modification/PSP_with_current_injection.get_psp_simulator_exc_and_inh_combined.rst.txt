PSP_with_current_injection.get_psp_simulator_exc_and_inh_combined
=================================================================

.. py:method:: simrun.PSP_with_cell_modification.PSP_with_current_injection.get_psp_simulator_exc_and_inh_combined(gExRange=[1.0], mode='synapses')

   Set up and combine excitatory and inhibitory PSP simulators.

   This method initializes two PSPs objects, one for excitatory and one for inhibitory postsynaptic potentials,
   and combines them into a single PSPs object.

   :Parameters: * **gExRange** (*list*) -- Range of excitatory conductance values to simulate (in :math:`\mu S`). Default: ``[1.0]``
                * **mode** (*str*) -- Mode of the simulation. Options:
                * **- ``'synapses'`` to activate individual synapses** (*default*)
                * **- ``'cells'`` to activate individual cells**

   :returns: Combined PSPs object with both excitatory and inhibitory components.
   :rtype: PSPs
