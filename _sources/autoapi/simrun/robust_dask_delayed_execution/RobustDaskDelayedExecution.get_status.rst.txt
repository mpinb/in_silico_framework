RobustDaskDelayedExecution.get_status
=====================================

.. py:method:: simrun.robust_dask_delayed_execution.RobustDaskDelayedExecution.get_status()

   :returns: A dictionary with the keys being the keys of the delayed objects and the values being the status of the computation.
