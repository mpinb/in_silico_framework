PSPs.get_synapse_coordinates
============================

.. py:method:: simrun.synaptic_strength_fitting.PSPs.get_synapse_coordinates(population, flatten=False, cell_indices=None)

   Get the coordinates of all synapses of a particular celltype.

   :Parameters: * **population** (*str*) -- The celltype to get the synapse coordinates from.
                * **flatten** (*bool*) -- Whether to flatten the list of synapse coordinates. Default: ``False``.
                * **cell_indices** (*list*) -- A list of cell indices to get the synapse coordinates from. Default: ``None`` (all synapses).

   :returns: A list of synapse coordinates.
   :rtype: list
