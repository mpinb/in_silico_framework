NetworkMapper._create_spiketrain_activities
===========================================

.. py:method:: single_cell_parser.network.NetworkMapper._create_spiketrain_activities(preCellType, networkParameters)

   Uses :py:meth:`~single_cell_parser.cell.PointCell.compute_spike_train_times` to calculate
