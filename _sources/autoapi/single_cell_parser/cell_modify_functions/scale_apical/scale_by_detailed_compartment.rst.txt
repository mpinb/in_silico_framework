scale_by_detailed_compartment
=============================


.. py:function:: single_cell_parser.cell_modify_functions.scale_apical.scale_by_detailed_compartment(cell, **kwargs)

   Scales subcellular compartments based on ``detailed\_labels``.

   If not yet assigned, detailed labels are assigned by :py:meth:`biophysics\_fitting.utils.augment\_cell\_with\_detailed\_labels`.
   and include ``basal``, ``trunk``, ``tuft``, and ``oblique``.

   .. attention::

      For non-L5PT neurons or L5PT neurons from any other brain area than barrel cortex,
      make sure :py:meth:`~biophysics\_fitting.utils.augment\_cell\_with\_detailed\_labels`
      assigns labels as you want them to be.
      
      Alternatively, assign them manually with the :py:attr:`biophysics\_fitting.cell.Cell.sections.label\_detailed` attribute.

   :Parameters: * **cell** (:py:class:`~single\_cell\_parser.cell.Cell`) -- The cell to scale.
                * **\*\*kwargs** (*dict*) -- Detailed lables with associated scaling factors.

   :returns: The scaled cell.
   :rtype: :py:class:`~single\_cell\_parser.cell.Cell`
