SpikeInit.analyze_single_spike
==============================

.. py:method:: single_cell_parser.analyze.membrane_potential_analysis.SpikeInit.analyze_single_spike(cell, tVec, thresh)
   :staticmethod:


   Calculate spike height, width (FWHM) and after hyperpolarization depth (AHP).

   Only does this for a single spike in the voltage trace: the one with the
   maximum deflection from the threshold.

   :Parameters: * **cell** (:py:class:`~single_cell_parser.cell.Cell`) -- Cell object.
                * **tVec** (*list*) -- Time vector.
                * **thresh** (*float*) -- Spike threshold.

   :returns: Tuple containing the spike height, full width half max (FWHM), and after hyperpolarization (AHP).
   :rtype: tuple
