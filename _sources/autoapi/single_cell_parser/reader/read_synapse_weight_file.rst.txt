.. title:

read_synapse_weight_file
========================


.. py:function:: single_cell_parser.reader.read_synapse_weight_file(fname)

   Reads list of all anatomical synapses and their maximum conductance values.

   :Parameters: **fname** (*str*) -- Synapse weight filename.
                See: :py:meth:`~single\_cell\_parser.writer.write\_synapse\_weight\_file`.

   :returns: two dictionaries with cell types as keys, ordered the same as the anatomical synapses:
             1st with section ID and pt ID, 2nd with synaptic weights, coded as dictionaries
             (keys=receptor strings) containing weights: (gmax\_0, gmax\_1, ... , gmax\_n)
   :rtype: tuple
