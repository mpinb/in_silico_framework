ScalarField.get_voxel_bounds
============================

.. py:method:: singlecell_input_mapper.singlecell_input_mapper.scalar_field.ScalarField.get_voxel_bounds(ijk)

   :Parameters: **ijk** (*tuple*) -- tuple of 3 integers: the indices of voxel in mesh.
