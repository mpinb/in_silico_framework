voltage_base
============


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.ephys.voltage_base(t, v, stim_delay)

   Calculates the mean voltage between 0.5 * stim\_delay and 0.75 * stim\_delay.

   :Parameters: * **t** (*numpy.ndarray*) -- Array of time values.
                * **v** (*numpy.ndarray*) -- Array of voltage values.
                * **stim\_delay** (*float*) -- Delay time of the stimulus.

   :returns: Mean voltage between 0.5*stim\_delay and 0.75*stim\_delay.
   :rtype: float
