BPAPatt
=======


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.ephys.BPAPatt(t, v_dend, thresh=&#39;+2mV&#39;, stim_onset=None)

   Computes the amplitude of the backpropagating action potential (bAP) at the dendrite.

   :Parameters: * **t** (*array*) -- Time array.
                * **v\_dend** (*array*) -- Dendritic voltage array.
                * **thresh** (*str or float*) -- Threshold voltage for detecting the bAP. Default is '+2mV'.
                * **stim\_onset** (*float*) -- Time of the stimulus onset. Default is ``None``.

   :returns: Amplitude of the bAP at the dendrite.
   :rtype: float
