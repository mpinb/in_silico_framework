STEP_mean_frequency
===================


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.ephys.STEP_mean_frequency(t, v, stim_duration=2000, thresh=None)

   Computes the mean frequency of action potentials in a voltage trace for a step stimulus.

   :Parameters: * **t** (*numpy.ndarray*) -- Array of time values.
                * **v** (*numpy.ndarray*) -- Array of voltage values.
                * **thresh** (*float, optional*) -- Voltage threshold for spike detection. Defaults to None.

   :returns: Mean frequency of action potentials in the voltage trace.
   :rtype: float
