augment_ddf_with_PCA_space
==========================


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.exploration_from_seedpoint.RW_analysis.augment_ddf_with_PCA_space(ddf)

   Augment a dask dataframe with the first two principal components of the hz current space.

   The hz current space is the space of current utilization during the BAC stimulus.
   The PCA space is the space spanned by the first two principal components of this space.

   :Parameters: **ddf** (*dask.dataframe*) -- the dataframe to augment

   :returns: the augmented dataframe, containing the additional columns: 'pc0', 'pc1', 'depolarization\_index', 'hyperpolarization\_index'
   :rtype: dask.dataframe
