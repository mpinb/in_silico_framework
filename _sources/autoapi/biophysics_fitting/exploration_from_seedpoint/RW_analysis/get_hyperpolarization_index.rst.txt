get_hyperpolarization_index
===========================


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.exploration_from_seedpoint.RW_analysis.get_hyperpolarization_index(dataframe)

   Compute the relative difference in hyperpolarization contribution of Im and SK during the BAC stimulus.

   This index is defined as:

   .. math::

       \frac{I\_{SK} - I\_{m}}{I\_{SK} + I\_{m}}

   :returns: the hyperpolarization index for each model in the dataframe
   :rtype: pd.Series
