get_depolarization_index
========================


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.exploration_from_seedpoint.RW_analysis.get_depolarization_index(dataframe)

   Compute the relative difference in depolarization contribution of Ca\_LVA and Ca\_HVA during the BAC stimulus.

   This index is defined as:

   .. math::

       \frac{Ca\_{LVA} - Ca\_{HVA}}{Ca\_{HVA} + Ca\_{LVA}}

   :Parameters: **dataframe** (*pd.DataFrame*) -- the dataframe containing the biophysical parameters

   :returns: the depolarization index for each model in the dataframe
   :rtype: pd.Series

   .. note:: This method is specific to L5PT neurons, or any cell with Ca\_LVA and Ca\_HVA channels.
