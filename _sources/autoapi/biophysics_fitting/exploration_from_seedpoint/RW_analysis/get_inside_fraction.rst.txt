get_inside_fraction
===================


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.exploration_from_seedpoint.RW_analysis.get_inside_fraction(l)

   Print the fraction of models that are inside the objective space.

   The exploration results contain a column "inside" that specifies whether or
   not a model is inside the objective space, i.e. below all thresholds for the objectives.

   :Parameters: **l** (*Load*) -- the Load object containing the exploration results

   :returns: None. prints out the fraction of models that are inside the objective space.
