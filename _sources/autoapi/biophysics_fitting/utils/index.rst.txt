.. autoapi-nested-parse::
   Utility functions for biophysics fitting.

   This module contains utilities and convenience methods for:
   - Selection sections
   - Reading out Vm at a section
   - Multiprocessing

   :widths: auto

   * - :py:obj:`get_inner_sec_dist_dict <biophysics_fitting.utils.get_inner_sec_dist_dict>`\ (cell, beyond\_dist, beyond\_struct, n\_children\_required)

   * - :py:obj:`augment_cell_with_detailed_labels <biophysics_fitting.utils.augment_cell_with_detailed_labels>`\ (cell)
     - Augment section labels to discriminate the tuft, oblique, trunk and basal dendrites.
   * - :py:obj:`tVec <biophysics_fitting.utils.tVec>`\ (cell)
     - Convenience method to convert a py:attr:`~single_cell_parser.cell.Cell.tVec` to a numpy array.
   * - :py:obj:`vmSoma <biophysics_fitting.utils.vmSoma>`\ (cell)
     - Convenience method to extract the soma voltage trace from a cell
   * - :py:obj:`vmMax <biophysics_fitting.utils.vmMax>`\ (cell)
     - Calculate the maximum voltage of a cell at any timepoint, at any dendrite.
   * - :py:obj:`vmApical <biophysics_fitting.utils.vmApical>`\ (cell, dist)
     - Fetch the membrane voltage of the apical dendrite at a certain distance from the soma.
   * - :py:obj:`vmApical_position <biophysics_fitting.utils.vmApical_position>`\ (cell, dist)
     - Fetch the exact location on the apical dendrite at a certain distance from the soma.

     - Execute a function in a child process.
   * - :py:obj:`pool_helper <biophysics_fitting.utils.pool_helper>`\ (callable\_partial)
     - Unserialize a function and its arguments, run it, and serialize the output.
   * - :py:obj:`execute_in_child_process_kept_alive <biophysics_fitting.utils.execute_in_child_process_kept_alive>`\ (fun)
