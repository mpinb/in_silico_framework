setup_StepThree
===============


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.setup_stim.setup_StepThree(cell, delay=700)

   Setup a step current stimulus at the soma:

       - amplitude = 1.507 nA
       - delay = 700 ms
       - duration = 2000 ms

   :Parameters: * **cell** (:py:class:`~single\_cell\_parser.cell.Cell`) -- The cell object.
                * **delay** (*float*) -- The delay of the step current stimulus (ms).

   :returns: None. Adds a step current stimulus to the cell.
