setup_bAP
=========


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.setup_stim.setup_bAP(cell, delay=295)

   Setup a bAP (backpropagating action potential) stimulus for the cell.

   Soma:

   - shape = step
   - amplitude = 1.9 nA
   - delay = 295 ms
   - duration = 5 ms

   :Parameters: * **cell** (:py:class:`~single\_cell\_parser.cell.Cell`) -- The cell object.
                * **delay** (*float*) -- The delay of the bAP stimulus (ms).

   :returns: None. Adds a bAP stimulus to the cell.
