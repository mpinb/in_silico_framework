_append
=======


.. py:function:: biophysics_fitting.setup_stim._append(cell, name, item)

   Append an item to a :py:class:`~single\_cell\_parser.Cell.cell` object.

   This is used to add e.g. injection/recording pipettes to the cell.

   :Parameters: * **cell** (*object*) -- The cell object.
                * **name** (*str*) -- The name of the attribute to append to.
                * **item** (*object*) -- The item to append.

   :returns: None. Adds :paramref:`item` to the :paramref:`cell` under the name :paramref:`name`.
