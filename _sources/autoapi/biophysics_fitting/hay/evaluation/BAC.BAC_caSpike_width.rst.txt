BAC.BAC_caSpike_width
=====================

.. py:method:: biophysics_fitting.hay.evaluation.BAC.BAC_caSpike_width(voltage_traces)

   Get the width of the calcium spike in the dendritic voltage trace.

   :Parameters: **voltage_traces** (*dict*) -- dictionary with the voltage traces of the soma and dendrite.

   :returns:

             Dictionary mapping ``".check_1_Ca_AP"`` to whether there is at least one calcium spike in the dendritic voltage trace,
                 and ``".raw"`` to the width of the calcium spike in the dendritic voltage trace.
   :rtype: dict
